Welcome to Maadi Community Church

For more than 70 years expatriates from all over the world have gathered together in Maadi, the beautiful garden suburb of Cairo, to worship God in English. Our main worship service takes place on Friday evenings from 5:00pm-6:15pm outside under a tent on the property of St. John the Baptist Anglican Church. A nursery and children’s program is offered during the service and many other programs and classes run throughout the week. We would love to have you visit us in person and be a part of this unique body of believers.
